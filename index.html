🐾 New Owner Tips (All Pets)

  • Dogs: Crate training, positive reinforcement, daily walks.
  • Cats: Scratching posts, playtime, slow introductions.
  • Parrots: Daily interaction, foraging toys, safe environment.
  • Small pets: Unlimited hay (rabbits/guinea pigs), gentle handling, spacious enclosure.
  • Always: Fresh water, quality food, regular vet care.
